Getting Started

Creating an eBook involves the following steps:
  • Creating a new project
  • Creating a series of pages and organizing them (i.e. into the chapters, sections and sub-sections of a book)
  • Publishing the eBook so your audience can view it
It is important to remember, however, that eBooks are not just about text - pages can contain interactive elements, like quizzes, or multimedia elements, like graphics, presentations or even audio and video. When building an eBook, be sure to take advantage of these capabilities at every opportunity - research has shown that a picture truly is worth a thousand words to your audience.
