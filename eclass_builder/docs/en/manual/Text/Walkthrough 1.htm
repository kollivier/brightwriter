In this tutorial, we will step through the process of creating an eBook so that you can see the steps involved. The basic outline of the eBook creation process is as follows:

  1. Create a New Project: Name your project and (optionally) provide some simple metadata.
  2. Add/Edit Pages: Just add a new page and start typing your content!
  3. Publish your eBook: Publish your eBook to CD-ROM or the Internet
