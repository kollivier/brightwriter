When starting to learn EClass.Builder, it may be easy to think of it as a visual 'book building' software package. Creating an EClass involves the following steps:
  • Creating a new project
  • Creating a series of pages and organizing them (i.e. into the chapters, sections and sub-sections of a book)
  • Publishing the EClass to CD-ROM or the Internet so your audience can view it.
It is important to remember, however, that EClass is not just about text - pages can contain interactive elements, like quizzes, or multimedia elements, like graphics, presentations or even audio and video. When building an EClass, be sure to take advantage of these capabilities at every opportunity - research has shown that a picture truly is worth a thousand words to your audience.
