Getting Started

Creating an EClass involves the following steps:
  • Creating a new project
  • Creating a series of pages and organizing them (i.e. into the chapters, sections and sub-sections of a book)
  • Publishing the EClass so your audience can view it
It is important to remember, however, that EClasses are not just about text - pages can contain multimedia elements, like graphics, presentations or even audio and video. The next few sections will help you get started.
